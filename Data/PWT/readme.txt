This is the Penn World Tables economic dataset, version 10.0

Metadata is available in the pwt10-0.xlsx file

More information is available at: https://www.rug.nl/ggdc/productivity/pwt/?lang=en

The reference paper for this dataset, with the most useful information about it, is Feenstra et al. (2015)

Feenstra, Robert C., Robert Inklaar and Marcel P. Timmer (2015), "The Next Generation of the Penn World Table" American Economic Review, 105(10), 3150-3182
